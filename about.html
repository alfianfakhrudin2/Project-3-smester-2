About Speednet

Speednet Indonesia was established in 2016 and is 100% privately owned We provide a wide range of telecommunications services for the Telecommunications, Government, Enterprise, Financial Services, Hospitality, Educational Institutions, Retail, Transportation, Public Sector, Military, Police, Defense, Manufacturing and more industries. Speednet is operated by experienced professionals with full ISP license.

We don't believe that cost efficiency, flexibility, quality of service and time are necessities for our customers, so we do our best to ensure the values that are felt by our customers and partners. Our goal is to provide complete resources to meet customer needs, the availability of speednet resources will help Customers to get the best solution. We intend to grow together with our Customers, Partners and Employees.

Our Vission

  • to be the leading provider of internet services ranging from simple internet services for the family, right up to being the experts that can be counted on for providing complex communication systems for any sized business.
  • continual growth and improvement without distorting or losing our values. We want to remain competitive and unique. We are developing so that our brand and market position is synonymous with the quality of our network.
  • High-speed internet access is now a necessity, not a luxury. Availability of superior telecommunications service is as fundamental to the future of our communities as was electricity in the last century. Local initiative is critical to ensure success.
  • We need a network capable of meeting the current and future bandwidth needs of residents, businesses, schools, health care providers, nonprofits and governments.
  • We need a network that will enable regional commerce, be financially sustainable, retain revenues from telecommunications services in the region, and will continue to do so in the future.

Our Mission

  • Deliver clear and simple product/plan innovations periodically to ensure we continue to provide customers with the most advanced telecommunications offerings available at a competitive price.
  • Provide a high level of service in our interactions with our internal and external customers, networks, systems and processes that deliver Best in Class customer satisfaction with a Likelihood of Recommendation score greater than 80%.
  • Operate with integrity to achieve a superior customer experience via clear, simple and streamlined business processes and an attitude which puts the customer first in everything we do.
  • Foster a culture of passionate, highly skilled and highly engaged people whose actions and attitudes are the basis of our enduring success. Employee engagement shall exceed industry benchmarks.
  • Ensure consistency, simplicity and scalability in our service delivery to maximize customer growth and retention opportunities.
  • Act in a way which is honest, fair, and responsible ensuring sustainable long term business practices.
  • Always developing and improving human resources consistently to achieve 100% customer satisfaction.
